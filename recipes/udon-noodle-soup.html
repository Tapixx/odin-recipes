Udon Noodle Soup

Description

A simple but tasty take on the classic soup Naruto-Style.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Udon noodles
  • 300 ml of water
  • Dashi stock granules
  • 1 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Mirin
  • 1 Naruto
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 piece of Kakiage fritters
  • 1 Mitsuba(garnish)

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a pan, and bring to a boil.
  2. When it's boiling, put in the udon noodles and naruto, and bring back to a boil.
  3. Transfer to a bowl, and top with kakiage, mitsuba and so on.
