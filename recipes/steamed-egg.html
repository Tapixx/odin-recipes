Steamed Egg

Description

A basic repice for steamed eggs made Naruto-Style.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Half a pack of Naruto(sliced thinly)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Water(similar in mass to eggs)

Steps

  1. Beat egg with salt. Then strain it.
  2. Beat again then add Naruto and water.Beat well.
  3. Steam it for 15 minutes.
